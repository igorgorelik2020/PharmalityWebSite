COMPANY HISTORY

Pharmality Enterprises, Inc. began in 2002 as an educational company focusing on delivering the latest pharmaceutical developments to a variety of target audiences. Combining our wealth of knowledge with rigorous training we sought to perfect our speaking methods and expand from mere delivering comprehensive information to motivating and inspiring enthusiasm within the audience towards a particular topic. While we perfected our presentation techniques we relied on outside companies to deliver Audio-Visual equipment to aid in delivering our message. Needless to say, the outsourced equipment failed us more often than not. Due to the failed equipment our skillfully crafted message was neither seen, nor rememberd.

 It became imperative for our company to create a new branch, dedicated to Audio-Visual equipment and technical support. Being an educational company gave us a unique perspective on understanding the needs of the speakers and the ability to technologically master the presentation. We were fortunate to hire experienced and dedicated technologically skilled staff to compliment our knowledgeable medical consultants. Central to our success for over two years is the wealth of experience and knowledge of our people and a strong client base that consistently chooses Pharmality Enterprises for all their medical educational and / or Audio-Visual needs.
